Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Wayfull ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Wayfull app.

Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password.

Grocery lists: We store the grocery lists and items you create in the app, including item names and aisle information.

Location data: With your permission, we access your device's location to detect when you arrive at a nearby grocery store. Location data is used only in real time and is not stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Wayfull app
  • Sync your grocery lists across sessions
  • Detect your nearest grocery store to sort items by aisle
  • Enable list sharing between users

Third-Party Services

Supabase: We use Supabase to store your account information and grocery lists securely. Supabase is SOC 2 compliant. Learn more at supabase.com/privacy.

Kroger API: We use the Kroger API to retrieve product aisle information for your local store. No personal information is shared with Kroger.

Anthropic Claude API: We use Claude to parse recipe ingredients when you use the recipe import feature. Only the ingredient text you paste is sent — no personal information is shared.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption. Your password is never stored in plain text. We use Row Level Security to ensure you can only access your own data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the third-party services listed above, which are necessary to operate the app.

List Sharing

When you share a grocery list using a share code, the recipient can view and add items to that list. Only people you explicitly share your code with can access your shared lists.

Location Data

Wayfull requests access to your location only while the app is in use ("When In Use" permission). Location data is used solely to detect nearby Kroger-family stores and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at privacy@wayfull.app. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

Children's Privacy

Wayfull is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@wayfull.app.
